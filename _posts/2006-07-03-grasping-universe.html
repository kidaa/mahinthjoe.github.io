--- layout: post title: Grasping the Universe date: '2006-07-03T08:48:00.000+05:30' author: Mahinth Christensen tags: modified_time: '2006-07-03T08:55:23.520+05:30' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-17838460.post-115189712351138490 blogger_orig_url: http://mahinth.blogspot.com/2006/07/grasping-universe.html --- The human mind is not capable of grasping the universe. We are like little child entering a huge library. The walls are covered to the ceilings with books in many different tongues. The child knows someone must have written these books. It does not know who of how. It does not understand the languages in which they are written. But the child notes a defenite plan in the arrangement of the books - a mysterious order which it does not comprehend, but only dimly suspects.
Albert Einstein
